WALABI Strategy 2025: Turning Digital Innovation into Impact for Agri-Food

At WALABI – Wageningen Lab for Agri-Food Business Informatics – 2025 is all about moving from vision to action. We know digital technologies are advancing at lightning speed, from generative AI and digital twins to data spaces and blockchain. But in agri-food, innovation is only meaningful if it creates real impact for people, businesses, and the planet.

That philosophy sits at the heart of our 2025 strategy. Here’s how we’re putting it into practice.

1. Knowledge Made Accessible

We’re transforming our expertise into bite-sized, attractive knowledge products – from 2-pagers and infographics to podcasts and video explainers. Why? Because decision-makers don’t just need theory, they need actionable insights. The new WALABI Academy will be the home for these resources, making complex topics like interoperability, smart business systems, and AI both understandable and usable.

2. A Breeding Ground for Digital Innovation

WALABI is a collaborative lab. We explore emerging digital trends, design and test prototypes, and co-create solutions with our partners. Our role is to act as a breeding ground for digital innovations – experimenting, testing, and translating ideas into scalable applications. From digital maturity scans to generative AI exploration, our services give organizations a safe space to explore the future.

3. Four Strategic Themes

Digital Trends Exploration

scouting technologies like AI, data spaces, and IoT, and translating them into practical applications.

Business Architecture

designing integrated frameworks that align strategy, processes, and IT.

Smart Business Information Systems

making systems smarter, more connected, and more transparent across the food chain.

Interoperability & Data Sharing

building fair digital infrastructures where data can be exchanged with trust and meaning.

4. Building Partnerships and Ecosystems

We’re not doing this alone. WALABI 2025 is about working with launch customers, research groups, and policy partners inside and outside Wageningen. We’ll be present at key events, run collaborative workshops, and invite stakeholders into our growing ecosystem. Because digital innovation only scales when it’s built together.

5. Our Commitment for 2025

  • More knowledge products, easier to access.
  • New ideation and advisory services for partners.
  • Stronger outreach through events, newsletters, and our LinkedIn page.
  • And above all: solutions that embed sustainability, transparency, and resilience into agri-food systems.

Looking Ahead

WALABI’s strategy for 2025 is ambitious, but grounded: it’s about making digital innovation work for agri-food. We’ll continue to balance scientific rigor with practical tools, building systems that are ready for the future.

Join us on this journey. Let’s turn data into decisions – and decisions into sustainable futures.
